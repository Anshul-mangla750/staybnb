Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a stay?

To book a stay, search for your desired location, select your travel dates, and choose from the available listings. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. Please check the payment options available during the booking process.

How can I contact a host?

You can contact a host by sending them a message through the platform. Simply go to the listing and click on the "Contact Host" button.

What should I do if I have a problem with my booking?

If you encounter any issues with your booking, please reach out to our customer support team for assistance.
